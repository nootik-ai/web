2. Use it on Google Meet

When you open a Google Meet, Nootik activates automatically.

  • Make sure captions are turned on (for real-time transcription).
  • The floating panel will appear in the bottom-right corner.
  • You‚Äôll see participants, transcriptions, and your distraction counter.
  • Leaving the tab increases the distraction count.

3. Get Real-Time Insights

When you get distracted or leave the meeting tab:

  • Nootik logs when you lost focus and resumed.
  • When you return, it generates a short AI summary of what you missed.
  • If you were mentioned by name, it highlights that moment.
  • You can save your full meeting transcript locally with one click.

4. Focus Mode & Privacy

  • Blocks web.whatsapp.com automatically while in a meeting.
  • All detection runs locally in your browser ‚Äî no data is stored externally.
  • Summaries use OpenAI securely for short text processing only.
  • You can disable features anytime from the panel.

Note: For best performance, keep captions enabled and avoid minimizing the Meet tab for long periods.
