Transparency and Privacy

We’re a small team building Nootik for people who struggle with focus. All processing is done locally whenever possible; we never share your audio without your consent. You can pause or delete your data at any time. We want you to know exactly what we’re building and how we use it.

Currently, Nootik works on Google Meet and Zoom (web version). We’re working to add more platforms.
