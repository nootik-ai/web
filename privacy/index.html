Privacy Policy

Atntiv is designed for PMs and Tech Leads who need clarity — without sacrificing privacy. This page explains exactly what we collect, what we don’t, and how your data is protected.

1. No Recordings. No Audio Stored.

Atntiv does not record, save, or stream audio from your meetings. We process real-time cues to detect requirement shifts and decision changes — but no conversation audio is ever stored on our servers.

2. What Atntiv Actually Analyzes

Atntiv uses a lightweight, privacy-first layer to understand when topics, decisions, or ownership change. We analyze:

  • High-level conversation signals (topic changes, decision phrases)
  • Speaker turns and context shifts
  • Your own interaction with the meeting (not your audio)

None of this is saved — it is computed quickly and forgotten.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No audio recordings
  • No video streams
  • No screenshots or screen recordings
  • No private meeting content stored on our servers
  • No access to your Google Meet account or calendar
  • No tracking across other websites

4. What Minimal Data We Store

To operate the beta, we store only:

  • Your email address (for beta access)
  • Basic technical logs (error reports, anonymized performance data)
  • Optional feedback you choose to share

Nothing tied to your meeting content is stored.

5. Local-first Processing

Atntiv is designed with a local-first architecture. Wherever possible, meeting context shifts are detected directly in your browser — not in the cloud.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your email and beta data at any time. Send a short message to:

team@atntiv.ai

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy as we improve Atntiv. When that happens, we’ll update the version and notify beta users.
